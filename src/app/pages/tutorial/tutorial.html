Welcome to ClassMate

The ClassMate - Student Buddy app is a practical preview of providing students with Lecture Notes & details necessary.

What is ClassMate?

The Classmate - Student Buddy App is a tool that enables students to access , download & learn from all the Lecture Notes , PDFs, PPTs, Resources of Expert Professors in our College.

What is the Appflow?

Its Simple .
First Register in the Classmate App , if already registered login to view your Schedule and Lecturers.
Click on any Subject , Resource , or any Lecturer to access their resources.
